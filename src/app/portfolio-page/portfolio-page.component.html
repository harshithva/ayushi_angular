OUR PORTFOLIO

Semester Registration

Semester registration web portal is made to digitalize the new semester registration system our college AKGEC. Through this portal, students can easily register themselves for the new semester. It also provides a faculty dashboard to keep track of total students, registered students, and remaining ones. It is very useful for faculty as it provides various facilities like create students, manage students, can see all the registered students, and branch wise summary of the total, registered and remaining students.

Kirana Hub

Kirana Hub app is an online grocery delivery service. It primarily delivers grocery goods found in convenience stores, home essentials and food supplies to its customers. It is made in order to facilitate the daily needs of the customers. Customers use a mobile application to order groceries online. Then secure the items from their warehouse and deliver the items to the consumer. The delivery may be scheduled for any time of the day.

Late Entry Portal

AKGEC Late entry system is an online portal which keeps a track of late entries. The dashboard shows the total count of users, students, and entries. An authenticated person can create or manage users, students, and entries as well. Users can analyze the daily reports. A final report is also maintained in the portal which can be sent to all HODs and Administrators in a particular format.

Jivin App

The Jivin App is real time mobile application based on their location in order to treat the patients with help of plasma therapy in the lieu of covid pandemic. Till date there is no vaccine successfully adapted to treat covid patients thus, this application provides an aid to the paitients by registering users to request and donate(from recovered covid-19 paitients) for plasma therapy. This is a platform made in order to lessen the hassle between the doctors and paitients in the current scenario. The app will help to overcome the existing challenge of finding the donor and save lives.

E-notice Board

E-notice Board is an online service that helps to keep track of all the notices issued by the college. It has two dashboards, one for faculty and another for an admin. Admins can upload calendar and can manage students and faculty. Faculty can create and update notices, can upload and view timetables, and can add new students. Portal also includes the list of holidays and academic calendar.

HealthCare7 App

The number of cases are increasing rapidly due to covid pandemic. In order to ease the current situation, we have created a healthcare7 mobile application which provides free delivery of sanitizing spray, stands and masks . In the light of this situation , this application provides free doorstep delivery all across India.The customers can place orders online and the delivery may be scheduled for any time of the day.
